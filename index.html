Our Initiatives

Life Care Charitable Trust is dedicated to making a positive impact in various areas:

Education

We believe in the power of education and provide support to ensure access to quality education for all.

Specially Abled

We support specially abled individuals with care, education, and employment opportunities.

Homecare

We offer homecare services to those in need, providing comfort and support in their own homes.

Homestay

Our homestay programs cater to the elderly, offering a safe and caring environment for their well-being.

Together, we strive to create a world where everyone has the opportunity to live a dignified and fulfilling life.
